WHO REALLY AM I?





I am a final-year Design student at the University of Aveiro, driven by curiosity, commitment, and a passion for visual communication. My main interests lie in graphic design, branding, and editorial design.

Beyond design, I am also an artist, working with drawing, painting, and digital illustration. During my academic journey, I have explored different areas of design, including product design, which helped me develop a broader and more holistic understanding of the creative process. I enjoy learning continuously, experimenting with new approaches, and seeing each project as an opportunity to grow both as a designer and as an artist.
