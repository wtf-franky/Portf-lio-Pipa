Brand Overview

Moss is a fictional coffee brand created to explore a unique and memorable identity in the coffee universe. The project includes visual identity, packaging design and mockup applications, reflecting,  quality, and authenticityin every detail.

Identity & Visual Language

Moss’s visual identity is modern, elegant and welcoming, using colors, typography, and graphic elements that evoke the sensory experience of coffee. The visual system is consistent and versatile, suitable for packaging, cups, and promotional materials.
